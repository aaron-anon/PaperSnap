## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does PaperSnap - PDF Scanner require an account?

No. PaperSnap - PDF Scanner does not require registration, login, or any user account.

### Does PaperSnap - PDF Scanner upload my documents?

No. PaperSnap - PDF Scanner does not upload your documents to any server. All scans remain on your device unless you choose to share or export them.

### Does PaperSnap - PDF Scanner collect personal data?

No. PaperSnap - PDF Scanner does not collect, track, store, or share personal information.

### What file formats can I export?

Scans can be exported as PDF, PNG, or JPG files.
