# PaperSnap - PDF Scanner

Professional document scanning in your pocket.

PaperSnap makes it easy to capture, convert, and share documents directly from your iPhone.

## Features

### High-Quality Scanning

Capture documents with automatic edge detection for clean and accurate scans.

### Scan Anything

Receipts, notes, contracts, invoices, forms, and more.

### Quick Conversion

Export scans as PDF, PNG, or JPG in seconds.

### Easy Sharing

Share documents instantly with a single tap.
